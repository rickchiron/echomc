Welcome To EchoMC

a brand-new 24/7 Lifesteal SMP server

built for players who love survival, PvP, and community events. Echo MC was inspired by the fan community of the legendary Lapata SMP and now returns with a fresh experience, improved features, and a growing player base.

Our server supports both Cracked and Premium players. It is compatible with Minecraft versions 1.8 – 1.21, making it accessible to almost everyone.

In Echo MC, every fight matters. With the Lifesteal system defeating other players lets you steal their hearts, making PvP intense and rewarding. Build your base, form alliances or dominate the battlefield — your story is up to you.

Server Features

  • Lifesteal SMP gameplay
  • Ranks and reward crates
  • Parkour challenges
  • PvP events and tournaments
  • Build competitions
  • End War battles
  • Friendly casual community
  • Smooth 24/7 uptime

Whether you enjoy grinding resources, fighting powerful enemies or showing off your building skills, Echo MC offers something for every type of player.
